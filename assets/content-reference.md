## 4. MyLinde

**Section Description:** Digital fleet management platform for complete visibility and control.

### Fleet Management & Analytics

#### Administration
- **Truck Management:** Centralized management of your entire truck fleet with real-time status tracking and maintenance scheduling.
- **Driver Management:** Complete driver authorization, training tracking, and performance management system.

#### Usage Control
- **PreOp Check:** Digital pre-operational checks ensuring safety compliance before every shift.
- **Shock Detection:** Real-time impact monitoring and reporting for damage prevention and accountability.

#### Fleet Optimization
- **Utilization Analysis:** Detailed usage data to right-size your fleet and eliminate idle equipment.

#### Service
- **Service Scheduling:** Automated maintenance scheduling based on usage hours and fleet needs.

### Service Management

#### Maintenance Planning
- **Preventive Maintenance:** Scheduled maintenance programs to maximize uptime and equipment lifespan.
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Data-driven maintenance predictions to prevent unplanned downtime.

#### Service Requests
- **Digital Service Portal:** Submit and track service requests through an intuitive digital platform.

#### Parts Management
- **Parts Availability:** Real-time parts inventory visibility and automated reordering.

### Safety Analytics / Guardian

#### Linde Safety Guard
- **Proximity Detection:** Warn and protect pedestrians and other vehicles in warehouse environments.
- **Speed Zones:** Automated speed reduction in designated high-risk areas.

#### Incident Reporting
- **Impact Analysis:** Comprehensive impact event tracking and root cause analysis.

#### Compliance
- **Safety Compliance Dashboard:** Real-time compliance monitoring and reporting for safety regulations.
